Who we are

Our vision is to see African individuals and families successfully settle, live and thrive in the UK. We serve the local African community in Sheffield and surrounding areas—providing a forum for interaction, integration and community cohesion.

Mission

• Make a difference through advocacy, education and employment support.
• Provide practical learning for navigating UK culture—family life, careers and education.
• Create a supportive environment to connect and share experiences.

What we do

Carefully curated events, workshops, webinars and digital resources keep our community informed about opportunities for learning, development and networking.

Workshops Webinars Networking

Focus

Supporting immigrants to integrate and thrive in the UK—with practical guidance, culturally-aware signposting, and peer support.

Our approach

We connect people to the right knowledge, the right networks and the right opportunities. From first-steps orientation to career development, we walk with you.
