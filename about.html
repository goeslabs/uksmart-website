About UKSmart

We are a Sheffield-based community group supporting African immigrants to live and thrive in the UK.

Vision

To educate, empower and advocate for the African community to successfully live and thrive in the UK.

Mission

  • Serve our local African community in Sheffield and the surrounding areas—creating a forum that promotes interaction, integration and community cohesion.
  • Make a difference through advocacy, education and employment support.
  • Provide practical learning for navigating cultural differences in family life, education and careers.
  • Create a supportive environment where individuals can connect and share experiences.

Approach

We inform our community about opportunities for learning, development and networking through carefully curated events, workshops, webinars, signposting and digital resources.

Our Story

UKSmart began as a grassroots effort to help new arrivals understand the systems, services and opportunities in the UK. Today, we work with local partners—including schools, universities, councils and employers—to deliver high‑impact sessions that change lives.
